Here’s a **beautiful site blueprint** for your product.

## Overall style direction

Think:

* clean like Stripe
* sharp like Vercel
* focused like Linear

Visual principles:

* lots of whitespace
* strong typography
* one accent color only
* subtle gradients, not loud ones
* rounded cards
* thin borders
* polished product mockups
* restrained motion

The feeling should be:

> technical, premium, calm, trustworthy

Not:

* busy
* corporate
* “AI magic”
* template-like

## Brand mood

Your product is infrastructure, so the design should communicate:

* reliability
* simplicity
* modern developer tooling

A good visual tone would be:

* white or very light gray background
* dark charcoal text
* muted secondary text
* one cool accent, like blue or violet
* occasional dark section for contrast

## Typography

Use a clean modern sans-serif.

Style hierarchy:

* big, bold hero headline
* concise supporting copy
* medium-weight section titles
* short card descriptions

Keep copy tight. Beautiful SaaS sites do not over-explain.

## Site map

Build only these pages:

* `/` homepage
* `/pricing`
* `/docs`
* `/demo`

That is enough.

## Design system guidance

### Colors

Use a restrained palette:

* background: white or near-white
* text: very dark gray
* secondary text: medium gray
* accent: blue or violet
* one dark section for code/API

### Spacing

Be generous.
The site should breathe.

### Buttons

Primary:

* filled
* dark or accent color

Secondary:

* ghost or outline

### Cards

* 16–24px padding
* subtle border
* soft shadow
* rounded corners

### Motion

Use very subtle motion only:

* fade in on load
* slight hover lift on cards
* gentle button hover

No heavy animation.
